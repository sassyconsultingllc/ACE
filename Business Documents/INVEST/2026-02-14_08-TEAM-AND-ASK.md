## Why This Team Will Win

### Technical Moat

The codebase is 76,996 lines of working Rust. Reproducing this from scratch would take 2-3 senior engineers 18+ months. The head start is real and growing with every commit.

### Architectural Advantage

Building on Chromium means inheriting Google's design decisions, telemetry hooks, and complexity. Building from scratch means every architectural choice serves the user, not an ad business.

### Market Timing

Google's Manifest V2 deprecation is pushing privacy users to look for alternatives. The window for a non-Chromium browser to capture this audience is now.

### Rust Ecosystem

The Rust crate ecosystem reached critical mass in 2024-2025. Building a browser in Rust is now feasible in a way it wasn't five years ago. Early mover advantage in Rust browser space.
