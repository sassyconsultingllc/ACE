### Lifetime Value (LTV)

| Tier | Monthly Rev | Avg Retention | LTV | LTV:CAC |
|------|----------:|:-------------|----:|--------:|
| Family | $4.99 | 24 months | $119.76 | 3.6x |

Target: LTV:CAC > 3x (healthy SaaS benchmark). Developer and Enterprise tiers are highly efficient.

## Key Metrics Dashboard (What We'll Track)

### Growth Metrics
- Daily/Weekly/Monthly Active Users (DAU/WAU/MAU)
- New user signups (daily)
- User retention (D1, D7, D30, D90)
- Referral rate (viral coefficient)

### Revenue Metrics
- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)
- Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)
- Revenue churn vs. logo churn
- Net Revenue Retention (NRR) — target >110%

### Product Metrics
- Daily browsing hours per user
- Features used per session
- Extension installs
- Family profile adoption rate
- Ad/tracker blocks per day (aggregate)

### Efficiency Metrics
- CAC payback period — target < 12 months
- Burn multiple (net burn / net new ARR)
- Revenue per employee
- Gross margin — target > 85% (software, no COGS)

## Sensitivity Analysis

### Optimistic Case (2x user growth)
- Year 3 Revenue: $9.6M
- Break-even: Month 14
- Seed round at Month 8

### Base Case (projections above)
- Year 3 Revenue: $4.8M
- Break-even: Month 22
- Seed round at Month 12

### Conservative Case (0.5x user growth)
- Year 3 Revenue: $2.4M
- Break-even: Month 30
- Seed round at Month 18

### Failure Threshold
- Below 5,000 users at Month 12 → pivot or acqui-hire conversations
- Below $10K MRR at Month 18 → reassess product-market fit
